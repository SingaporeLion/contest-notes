# Finding Quality Checklist

Before submitting:

- Is the behavior actually reproduced?
- Are the preconditions explicit?
- Is the root cause concrete?
- Is the impact real, not implied?
- Is the severity justified by the demonstrated result?
- Could this be judged as governance / ops / intended authority only?
- Did I separate confirmed behavior from likely root cause?
- Is the report easier to judge because of how it is written?
